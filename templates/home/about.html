Why does this exist?

This project was developed for the Recommendation System section of UCSD's Data Science capstone course.

The reason why our team chose this project in particular is that we are all avid music listeners and while brainstorming ideas we had a realization. Even though we all come from different family backgrounds and have different music preferences, at one point or another all had to have an uncomfortable conversation with our parents because of the music that we were listening to.
